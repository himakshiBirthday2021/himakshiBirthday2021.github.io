April 1, 2020

This was (obviously) at one of the poojas. My God!! Pujas at our home are so much fun. The delay, the chaos, the tension!! Impeccable! Badedaddi's on-off-on kuch kuch beech mein literally bas awaz nikal dena, badimumma ka constant "ek aur karlo", papa's impatience aur mummy toh khair chhodo unhe toh idhar udhar bhagte rehna hai bas.
